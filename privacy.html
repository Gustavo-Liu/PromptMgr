Prompt Manager Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2024

Data Collection

Prompt Manager does not collect any user data. All prompts are stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API.

Data Storage

All prompts are stored locally in your browser. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any servers.

Permissions

  • storage: Used to save prompts locally in your browser
  • clipboardWrite: Used to copy prompts to your clipboard
